How to Play MahJong Solitaire

Summary

MahJong Solitaire is played by removing the tiles from the board. This is done by removing any free tiles. Once all tiles are removed the player wins. It is not always possible to win every game of MahJong Solitaire.

Open Tiles

Open tiles are determined as tiles that have no other tile on top of them and no tile either to it's left or it's right. It may have one tile on either it's left or right but not on both sides.

Matching Tiles

There are 144 tiles and four of each tile with the exception of the season and flower tiles (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter, Plum, Chrysanthemum, Orchid, and Bamboo) of which there is only one each. Each tile can only match with another tile of the same type and rank with the expetion of the season and flower tiles for which any season tile will match with any other season tile and any flower tile will match with any other flower tile.
